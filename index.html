FRC Team 6324

At team 6324 we aspire to promote STEM education help build and grow robotics programs, and connect students and industry professionals in our community.

About Us

We are team 6324 from Salem, New Hampshire. Our rookie season was in 2017, and from then on we have worked to create a welcoming environment for anyone who wants to join the team. Part of having a welcoming environment is being involved in the FIRST® and local community. We value collaboration, gracious professionalism, and communication. One of our main goals for the members of the team is to prepare them for occupations in the science industry by practicing our values, as well as teaching them Scrum, graphic design and adobe, CAD and design, software development, public relations, outreach, and manufacturing.

FIRST

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) teams design, program, and build a robot starting with a standard kit of parts and common set of rules to play in a themed head-to-head challenge. Teams also build a brand, develop community partnerships for support, and work to promote STEM in their local community.

Outreach

We host outreach events to promote STEM and FIRST values in our community, allowing us to show what we do, and attract new team members and community partners. Previously, we have attended events such as:

  • Salem Scramble
  • Salembration
  • Our FLL summer camp
  • Salem Holiday Parade
  • Cars, Robots & Coffee
  • Woodbury Olympics

FLL summer camp

FIRST Lego League Summer Camp is a community outreach event that allows us to get involved with elementary level students and introduce them to STEM.
